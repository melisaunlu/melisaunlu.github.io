Magnetic Fields Modeling

Spent a summer working with Dr. Krista Soderlund, Dr. Chi Yan, and Dr. Dunyu Liu on numerically modeling magnetic fields around early Earth, Venus, and the Moon.

Generated magnetic and kinetic energy fields, zonal flows, and entropy levels of early Earth using supercomputers and parallel computing.

Successfully implemented and validated a radial electrical conductivity profile with a sharp gradient at the core–mantle boundary.

Conducted extensive test cases analyzing magnetic surfaces, tilt, zonal flows, and kinetic/magnetic field behavior.
