Magnetic Fields Modeling

Worked with Dr. Krista Soderlund, Dr. Chi Yan, and Dr. Dunyu Liu on numerical modeling of planetary magnetic fields.

Simulated magnetic and kinetic energy fields and analyzed zonal flows using high-performance computing systems.

Implemented radial electrical conductivity profiles with sharp core–mantle boundary gradients and validated multiple test cases.
