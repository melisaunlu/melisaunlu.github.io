Workshop Coordinator

As a workshop coordinator, I presented three workshops: a professional resume workshop with Cougarettes, a two-day Web Development workshop, and a MATLAB workshop. I created slides, trained incoming officers, collaborated with other organizations, and designed interactive materials for students.

The goal was to help students strengthen practical coding abilities while also preparing them for internships, research opportunities, and industry careers.
