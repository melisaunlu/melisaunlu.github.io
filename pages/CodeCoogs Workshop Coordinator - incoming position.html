Workshop Coordinator

As a workshop coordinator, I presented three workshops: a professional resume workshop with Cougarettes, a two-day comprehensive WebDev workshop, and a MATLAB workshop with Cougarettes. I created the slides, helped train the incoming workshop officer, collaborated with other officers and organizations, and designed interactive materials for students.

The goal was to help students strengthen their practical coding abilities while also preparing them for internships, research opportunities, and industry careers.
