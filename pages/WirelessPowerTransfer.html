Project Overview

  • Engineered a custom PCB featuring a 3-coil planar inductive array for contactless power transfer via electromagnetic coupling
  • Designed and soldered a crystal-driven oscillator with transistor amplification stages to drive resonant AC signals through the coil array
  • Tuned circuit parameters, including capacitance and coil geometry, to optimize coupling efficiency between transmitter and receiver coils
