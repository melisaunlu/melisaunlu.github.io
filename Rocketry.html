Rocketry

During high school, including the Covid period and Freshman year at University of Houston, I was active in AIAA-UH (American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics). I worked on two rockets: Oberon and Zephyr. As part of avionics and an officer, I developed flight computers, worked on telemetry, and co-founded the hobby rocketry team. I also conducted workshops on soldering and 3D printing.
